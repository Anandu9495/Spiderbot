Biomimetic spider bot

A biomimetic robot that can explore earth environment in dangerous and difficult places. Its inspired from the spider structure with 3 degree of freedom for each leg fabricated. The chosen structure gives the robot manoeuvrability on uneven surfaces thus able to search for survivors in wars, in natural disasters like earthquakes etc,.
